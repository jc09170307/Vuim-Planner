⚠️ Risks & Bottlenecks

"The warrior who knows every danger on the battlefield moves through it with confidence." — Sensei 🎋

🔑
CRITICAL
EAM303 — The Root Gate (Spring 2026)
Gates ALL herbology (HB310-HB550) AND multiple EAM courses. The single most important course in Year 1. If failed or delayed, the entire program cascades forward by multiple quarters.
⚔️ Action: Take Spring 2026. Never delay under any circumstance. Dedicate 60% of study time to this course.
🔒
CRITICAL
CPR / HIPAA / OSHA / EHR Certifications — Invisible Gates
These are hard stops. CPR/HIPAA/OSHA required BEFORE CO450 (Spring 2027). EHR required BEFORE CO510. CNT Certification required BEFORE CI510 (Spring 2028). Missing any one blocks clinical entry completely.
⚔️ Action: Complete CPR/HIPAA/OSHA NOW — set calendar reminders for annual renewals.
📝
CRITICAL
Phase Exam 1 — Gates Entire Clinical Career (Spring 2027)
Must pass Phase Exam 1 before CO450 clinical observation. If failed, entire internship chain delays by one quarter minimum. The Mambo break that never comes — everything stalls.
⚔️ Action: Begin Phase Exam 1 prep in Fall 2026 — two full quarters in advance. Treat every midterm as PE1 practice.
🌿
CRITICAL
HB310 + HB410 + HB420 + HB430 — All Four Before Any Formula
ALL four single-herb courses must complete before HB440/510/520/530. Each delay in any one of the four cascades into the formulas sequence. HB550 gates CI550 — the final internship.
⚔️ Action: Spread HB310→HB430 carefully across Fall 2026–Spring 2028. Never skip one.
🏥
CRITICAL
Summer 2028 — The Hardest Quarter in the Program
CI510 (3 clinical shifts/week) + EAM430 Internal Medicine I + EAM440 Internal Medicine II + HB440 Formulas I = ~19 credits. The most demanding quarter in the entire program. Zero flexibility.
⚔️ Action: Preview EAM430/440 during Spring 2028 break. Build weekly recovery routine. Do NOT add electives this quarter.
🔑
HIGH
WM420 Pathophysiology II — Gates 7 Courses (Winter 2027)
WM420 opens WM480, WM450, WM530, WM500, WM510, WM520, and WM550 simultaneously. Miss it and 7 doors stay closed for one full quarter.
⚔️ Action: Take Winter 2027. Never delay. This is as important as EAM303 for the Western Medicine track.
📊
HIGH
GPA Below 3.0 — Academic Probation Trigger
Hard minimum GPA is 3.0. Academic probation can affect financial aid, clinical eligibility, and graduation timeline. The 🔴 heavy quarters of 2028 are highest risk.
⚔️ Action: Monitor GPA every quarter. If approaching 3.2 — reduce elective load immediately. Never wait until after it drops below 3.0.
📅
MEDIUM
Course Availability — Some Courses Offered Once Per Year
Some courses are only offered in specific quarters. Missing registration means waiting a full year. The music only plays once — miss it and wait.
⚔️ Action: Register on the FIRST day of open registration every quarter. Set calendar reminders for all registration windows.
